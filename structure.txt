STRUCTURE

Overarching namespace 'game'.

src/cell - cell-building scope.
src/cell/organelle - buildings and routes.
src/cell/transport - walkers, drifters, interfaces.

src/gui - gui scope.
src/gui/game - things that plug into the main irrlicht window.
src/gui/view - things drawn onto the screen.
src/gui/view/gooey - things with buttons that can be clicked on.

src/world - strategy scope.
src/world/entity - all entities except scenery.
src/world/entity/resource - resources.
src/world/entity/damage - viruses, damage toxins.
src/world/scenery - contains both flow and scenery-entities.
